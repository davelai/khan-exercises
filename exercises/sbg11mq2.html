Zeros are the x-values where f(x)=0 (also known as x-intercepts).

f(x) = x^2 + B_DISPx + C can be factored as f(x) = (x+P)(x+Q).

Solving f(x)=0: (x+P)(x+Q)=0

Then (x+P)=0, or, (x+Q)=0

Therefore, x=-P, or, x=-Q

There are only two zeros: -P, or, -Q
